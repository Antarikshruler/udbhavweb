MUSIC EVENTS RULES

     Madhuri : 9739111276

1. Battle of Bands (Inter-College Event)
Alfred Samuel: 8802160108

  1. Maximum 6 singers in a band. Any number of instruments allowed.
  2. Maximum time allowed is 15 minutes + 5 minutes for stage setup.
  3. Judgment will be based on qualities like composition, rhythm, difficulty and general impression.
  4. No language restrictions. Fusion music is allowed and appreciated.
  5. Bands interested in registering have to send the videos and links to their material with the subject 'UdbhavBattleofBands​ ' to samarthmamadapur1@gmail.com
  6. Entries must be sent before April 16th, 2017. A maximum of 6 bands will be shortlisted.
  7. Pre-recorded music will not be allowed.

2. Western Acoustics Solo (Inter/Intra College Event)
Madhuri Suresh: 9739111276

     Vocal Solo
  1. This event will have two rounds, inter and intra. The intra college round will take place prior to the event and winners will compete in the second round which will be inter college.
  2. Maximum number of accompanists is two.
  3. Duration of the performance shall be between 4-6 minutes excluding setup time of 2 minutes.
  4. Judgment will be based on qualities like composition, rhythm, style, difficulty, fluency and general impression.

3. Western Acoustics Group
Madhuri Suresh: 9739111276

  1.  Maximum of 8 people in a group.
  2. Any number of instruments allowed. DRUMS WILL NOT BE PROVIDED.
  3. Only acoustic instruments to be used, keyboard can be used in piano mode only.
  4. Maximum time allowed for group song is 10 minutes + 5 minutes for stage setup.
  5. Judgment will be based on qualities like composition, rhythm, difficulty, coordination and general impression.
  6. Language must be English only.
  7. A Capella teams will be allowed.

4. Indian Light Music Solo (Intra/ Inter College Event)
Sahana: 9902679023

  1. Event will consist of two separate rounds – Qualifying round and Finals – which will be held on two separate days
  2. The qualifier round is an intra college event.
  3. Qualifying round will have a time limit of 3 minutes
  4. Final round will be an inter college event which consist of two individual rounds which will be decided by the judges. Finalists should be ready with 5 songs of preferably different genres
  5. Only film songs are allowed.
  6. Karaoke is not allowed for prelims as well as finals. The final rounds will be accompanied by a professional orchestra which shall be arranged.
  7. Inter college participants must send their entries (MP3 or MP4 audio/online links) to Pramod.gabbur@Gmail.com by 6:00 PM, 18th March.
  8. Judges decision is final and binding.

5. Classical Vocal solo (Inter-College Event)
Anirudh: 9845157211

  1. Duration of performance shall be 10-12 mins.
  2. Maximum number of accompanists is two
  3. Time for stage or instruments setting is maximum 3 mins.
  4. Item can be presented in either Hindustani or Carnatic style.
  5. Film songs are not allowed under this event.
  6. Sufficient thought and care must be exercised in the choice of raga and composition.
  7. Judgment will be based on qualities like taal, selection of raga, composition and general impression.

 6.  Semi-Classical Vocal Solo (Inter-College Event) (Intra-College Event)
Aanaga: 9986857765

  1. Time limit shall be 5 minutes.
  2. Time for stage or instruments setting is maximum 2 mins.
  3. Only non filmy songs /geeth/ghazal/bhajan/shabad and abhanges can be presented.
  4. A maximum of two accompanists allowed.
  5. Film songs not allowed.
  6. Judgment will be based on qualities like swara, taal, selection of raga, composition and overall presentation.
  7. Judges decision is final and binding.

 7. Classical Instrumental Solo (Inter-College Event)
Alfred Samuel: 8802160108

  1. Instrument can be percussion or non percussion.
  2. Duration of performance shall be 10-12 min.
  3. Maximum time for stage/instrument setup is 3 minutes.
  4. Maximum number of accompanists is two.
  5. Participants must bring their own instruments.
  6. Item can be presented in either Hindustani or Carnatic style.
