ART EVENT RULES

     Thressia : 9886495776

1. Cartooning

  1. Topic will be given on the spot.
  2. Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  3. Participants shall bring their own material.
  4. Drawing paper will be provided. (Size of paper : 22inches x 15 inches)

2. On The Spot Painting (Inter-College Event)

  1. Theme will be given on the spot.
  2. Duration will not be more than 2 hours 30 minutes.
  3. Size of the painting will be half imperial size drawing paper i.e 22 inches x 15 inches.
  4. Painting can be done in oil,water,poster or pastel colours.

Candidate shall bring their own material like brushes,paints etc. Only the paper or sheet will be provided.

3. Collage

  1. Topic will be given on the spot.
  2. Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  3. Participants shall bring their own scissors, pastes and other material required for the contest.
  4. Drawing paper will be provided. Size of drawing paper: 22inches X 15inches.
  5. Collage has to be prepared from old magazines or newspapers.
  6.  Participants are requested to carry their own newspapers, magazines and other decorating material.

4. Poster Making

  1. Event will be conducted on the spot and participants will be requested to do poster making on the subject or topic / theme giving by the judges/event coordinator.
  2. Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  3. Participants shall bring their own material. Only the drawing paper of size 22” x 15” will be provided by the organizers.

5. Clay Modeling

  1. Event will be conducted on the spot.
  2. Duration will not be more than 2 hours 30 minutes.
  3. Topics or size and other specific rules shall be announced on the spot.
  4. Clay shall be provided by the organizers.

6. Rangoli

  1. Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  2. Participants shall bring their own material. This art is known differently in various regions such as Mandna, Alpana, Alekhan, kolam, rangoli etc. For this the medium and form for expression can be freehand, pictorial and descriptive.
  3. Only one medium shall be used – Poster colors, or flower petals, or sawdust, or pulses, or rice without pasting.

7. Face Painting

  1. Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  2. Participants shall bring their own material.
  3. The participants have to bring their partners to paint on their respective partner’s face.
  4. Theme will be provided on the spot.

8. Mehendi

  1. Duration will not be more than 2 hours.
  2. The participants have to bring their partners to paint on their respective partner’s hands.
  3. The participants have to draw mehendi on both the hands of the partner.
  4. Mehendi cones will be provided.

9. Paper Couture

  1. There must be 2 members in a team.
  2. Theme will be given on the spot.
  3. The duration of the event will be not more than 2 hours.
  4. Participants are requested to carry their own material like staplers, tape and scissors, etc.

10. Camouflage

  1. There must be 2 members in a team.
  2. The theme for the event will be given on the spot.
  3. The duration of the event will be not more than 1 hour 30 minutes.
  4. Participants are requested to carry their own material like paints, brushes, markers    etc.

11. Flag Design

  1. Single or two member teams.
  2. The theme will be given on spot.
  3. Duration of the event will be 2 hours.
  4. Participants are requested to carry their own material like paints, brushes, markers etc.
  5. Cloth of 24" x 33" will be provided.
