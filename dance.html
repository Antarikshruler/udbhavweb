DANCE EVENT RULES

     Kruthika: 9964341820

1. Classical Solo
Sanchali: 9663798376 Arpitha: 9176909408

  1. The classical dance can be from any of the approved schools of dance such as Kathak, Kathakali, Bharath Natyam, Xatriya, Manipuri, Kuchipudi, Mohiniattam, Odissi etc.
  2. Participant will be allowed up to 15 minutes including time for preparation. Maximum three accompanists are permissible.
  3. Judgment will be based on the qualities like taal, technique, rhythm, abhinaya or expression, costumes, footwork and general impression, etc.
  4. Three copies of a brief note on the description of dance story involved in it, if any, and of the accompanying song with its meaning in Hindi or English must be submitted at the time of the event at the venue.

2. Dance As You Like (Inter-College Event)
Yash: 9461194687 Sakshi: 7353937963

  1. It is an individual event.
  2. No restrictions on the form of dance.
  3. Prelims consist of 1 round, in which the participant has to perform for a maximum of 3+1 minutes.
  4. Participants must get their own music. It should be provided in a CD/pen drive and should be submitted 1 hour prior to the event.
  5. Finals consist of 2 rounds. Rules will be announced on the spot.
  6. Judgment will be based on technique and style of dance.
  7. Obscenity in dress code will result in disqualification.
  8. Any form of vulgarity or indecent behaviour will lead to immediate disqualification and a possible ban from further RIT competitions.

3. Indian Group Dance (Inter-College Event)
Sanchali: 9663798376 Nisha: 9176909408

  1. Team shall consist up to 16 participants.
  2. The dance can be either Indian Style (Tribal/Folk), Contemporary or Bollywood.(No Classical dance forms allowed)
  3. Duration of a dance should not be more than 8+2 minutes.
  4. Participating teams will be responsible for removal of their sets/properties etc. immediately after the completion of performance.
  5. Judgment will be based on rhythm, formation, expression, costumes, make-up, sets, and on overall effect.
  6. Use of fire, explosives, dust etc. is strictly prohibited.
  7. Any form of vulgarity or indecent behaviour will lead to immediate disqualification and a possible ban from further RIT competitions.

4. Western Group Dance (Inter-College Event)
Nidhi: 9480075047 Kritika: 9964341820

  1. Team shall consist up to 16 participants.
  2. Time duration is 8+2 minutes.
  3. The participating team will be responsible for the removal of their sets/properties etc. immediately after the completion of the performance.
  4. Judgment will be based on choreography, costumes and overall presentation.
  5. A minimum of 4 dancers and a maximum of 16 dancers on stage at any particular time.
  6. Songs should be provided on a CD or pen drive containing only songs required by that performance.
  7. Props are allowed. Use of fire or other explosives strictly not allowed.
  8. Same person cannot dance in more than one dance of the same category.
  9. At no point of the sequence should the stage be left completely empty.
  10. No profanity is to be displayed.
  11. Any form of vulgarity or indecent behavior will lead to immediate disqualification and a possible ban from further RIT competitions.
  12. In case of number of registered teams exceeding the number of slots a screening process will take place, details of which will be given to the teams.

5. Street Dance (Inter-College Event)
Namratha: 9980317955 Andrea: 9945307401
(Non – Engineering crews are permitted to take part.)
Categories: Beatboxing; 2vs 2(all styles); 3vs 3(breaking); 7 to smoke(all styles)


        Beatboxing rules:
  1. This event will have three rounds.
  2. First round is a showcase round where the participant will perform solo. The performance shall be less than 3 minutes.
  3. Second round will be a battle round where each participant will face off against a randomly chosen opponent.
  4. The final round will be a battle between the two finalists selected by the judges.
  5. The judges decision is binding.
  6. Competitors should not perform beatbox routines as already performed in public by other beatboxers to the best of their knowledge.
  7. Inappropriate behavior will lead to disqualification.
  8. Solo: 1:00 showcase (eliminations). 1 minute throwdowns. 2 throwdowns Tie breakers 30 seconds.
  9. 7 to smoke: 1 minute showcase. Top 8 will be selected. 1 minute throwdowns Till one person gets 7 smokes. No instruments are used. Judge decision is final.

            1 vs 1 Breaking:
  1. Participants need to be present on time and always near the battle area.
  2. First round will consist of showcase.
  3. Other rounds will consists of Battles.
  4. Judges decision is final.
  5. Inappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.

            7 to smoke Krump::
  1. Participants need to be present on time and always near the battle area.
  2. First round will consist of Showcase.
  3. The next round is where they battle it out.
  4. Judges decision is final.
  5. Inappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.

            1 vs 1 Popping
  1. Participants need to be present on time and always near the battle area.
  2. First round will consist of showcase.
  3. The next is where they battle it out.
  4. Judge’s decision is final.
  5. Inappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.

            7 to smoke(All styles):
  1. The first round is the 'SHOWCASE' round. The participants will be grooving for the music played by the DJ.
  2. The participants will be eliminated based on this round.
  3. The next round is battle round where they battle it out.
  4.  If the scores are tied in the final round and judges are unable to decide, then there will be another tie breaker 'SHOWCASE' round for 1 min and the winner will be decided based on that. 
  5. Inappropriate behavior will lead to immediate disqualification.
