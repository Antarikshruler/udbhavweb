About UDBHAV

Every year, the students and faculty participate in a cultural extravaganza to showcase their talent and celebrate youth. This March, once again, RIT opens its arms to all colleges across South India, to experience what RIT is truly famous for. The abundance of talent and spirit!

Udbhav, the annual youth festival, boasts of events that are both nerve-racking, yet filled with fun and festivity!

About MSRIT

Ramaiah Institute of Technology was started in 1962 by the late Dr. M.S. Ramaiah, our Founder Chairman who was a renowned visionary, philanthropist, and a pioneer in creating several landmark infrastructure projects in India. Noticing the shortage of talented engineering professionals required to build a modern India, Dr. M.S. Ramaiah envisioned RIT as an institute of excellence imparting quality and affordable education.
