UDBHAV 2018

Udbhav is that eagerly awaited spectacle which acts as a platform for students from within and outside Karnataka to showcase their immense talent. Udbhav’s journey itself has been an adventurous one, from being a small cult celebration to now being one of the most awaited college fests in South India.

Over the years, Udbhav has been seen becoming a brand which attracts a lion’s share of fest-goers.

Having strongly associated with some of the best companies in India, Udbhav boasts itself as one of the best college fests in the garden city of Bangalore!

This Udbhav sees just one difference, everything gets better! We are going all out, to keep Udbhav at the top of the charts.
