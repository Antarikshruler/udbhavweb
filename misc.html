MISCELLANEOUS EVENTS RULES

     Sudip : 9591745903

  1. Personality
    Prelims
    1. Questionnaire to be collected from Pit Stop on 20th of April.
    2. Form has to be submitted on the same day.
    3. You have to be creative, humorous and make sense.
    4. No profanity is allowed.

    Finals
    1. Finalists will be announced on the morning of the day of the event.
    2. All rounds will be announced by the coordinators/judges during the course of the finals.
    3. Decision of the judges is final and binding.
    4. No profanity is allowed.

  2. MSRIT's Got Talent
    1. It is an individual or a group of participants of maximum 5 people event, which showcases the talents of performers in music, dance, acting, art, or any other creative performance. (In case of more than one participant for an act the participants need to inform the event coordinator during registration).
    2. It is strongly recommended that an act runs no more than 3 minutes 15 seconds. Under no circumstances may an act run more than 3 minutes 15 seconds. Acts exceeding time limit will be disqualified.
    3. The act is timed from the first initiation of the act.
    4. The participants must report at least an hour before the start of the event at the venue.
    5. CD player will be provided. Events where music is required the participants need to submit the CD’s by the specified date at the PITSTOP. Please label your CD’s with name and USN for identification.
    6. No dangerous props like fire, glass, etc can be used during any of the performances.
    7. No props will be provided.
    8. There will be two rounds in the event. All contestants must perform a different piece for finals than the one they performed for the preliminary show.
    9. No vulgarity is allowed.

  3. On the Spot Photography (Inter-College Event)
    1. The participant has to bring his/her own camera.
    2. The camera should have a memory card which will be formatted before the commencement of the contest.
    3. The participant has to submit 5 photographs on the theme announced on the spot.
    4. Basic post processing of the images like colour correction, cropping will be allowed. Usage of filters, morphing,cloning is not permitted.
    5. If the participant wishes to post process his image he needs to bring his own software. No software will be provided by us.
    6. Images are evaluated on the basis of
      1. Impact
      2. Composition
      3. Technical quality
      4. Suitability for the specific theme.
    7. Additional rules and theme will be announced on the spot.
    8. Vulgar, obscene or questionable photographs will be dealt with seriously.
    9. Additional rules will be announced on the spot.

  4. Treasure Hunt
    1. Event will be conducted within college premises.
    2. All participants must assemble at the venue 30 minutes before the start of the event.
    3. Maximum number of participants is 3 per team.

  5. Cooking
    1. A maximum of two members per team.
    2. Total preparation time is 2 hours.
    3. The participants are required to bring the necessary ingredients for cooking.
    4. Participants are required to bring the necessary utensils and cutlery.
    5. All the dishes should be vegetarian.
    6. The participants are required to make a starter/ drink, a main course and a dessert.
    7. The participants should bring separate glasses, spoons, straws, etc. for the judges.
    8. Packaged drinks are not allowed.
    9. Heating by any method is not permitted.
    10. Pre cooked items are not allowed
    11. Cutting/peeling of fruits or vegetables should be done on spot.
    12. Participants are expected to report 15 minutes prior to the commencement of the competition.
    13. The theme will be announced on the day of the competition.

  6. Dota 2
    1. Each team will consist of 5 members.
    2. Each game will end in Elimination of losing team, Except for the final, which will be best of three matches.
    3. Round 1 and Round 2 will be ranked all pick. Semi-finals and Finals will be captains mode. Finals will be a best of three series.
    4. GG can be called any time and the match may be forfeited.
    5. Fixtures will be decided beforehand and time slots will be provided. Each team is expected to be present 1 hour before their match or else it may lead to disqualification. 
    6. All 3rd party softwares, hacks and scripts are banned. The match will be monitored closely by the host server and umpires to ensure that the games are fair.
    7. There will be a coin toss to deciPauses without any valid reason won’t be entertained
    8. No substitutions of team mates can take place without a valid explanation and the consent of the event organizer.
    9. No substitutions of team mates can take place without a valid There will be a coin toss to decide which team is on the Radiant and the Dire. And most importantly, Come up with a cool team name and have fun.

  7. Fifa 15
    1. Game type: Friendlies. 
         Half length: 5 minutes. 
         Controller will NOT be provided. Each participant must bring their own. 
         No 5-star teams allowed. 
         In case of a draw, winner is decided as follows: 
         i) Final : Extra Time ii) Semi-final : Penalty Shootout iii) Other games : Golden Goal 

  8. Counter Strike Global Offensive
    1. The Organizer’s decision is final.
    2. Team of 5.
    3. This is a direct elimination (Knockout) tournament.
    4. The participants can get their own gaming gear.
    5. The PCs are equipped with GTX970 and Benq 144 hz monitors.
    6. The game version is the latest one which will be running on offline mode.
    7. Participants will not be allowed to login to their steam profiles as they will be provided by us
    8. Maps available for veto will be from Active duty pool including Dust 2
    9. On spot registrations are allowed
    10. Gear Provided by us are as stated below
      1. Mouse : Dragon War Thor
      2. Keyboard : Dragon War Desert Storm
      3. Headphones : Dragon War
      4. Mousepad : Dragon War (Speed)
    11. Rules For Preliminary Rounds :
      1. Preliminary Rounds will be of Overtime format where the game will be for 10 rounds (Race to 6)
      2. The Startmoney for Prelims will be 10,000$
      3. The map will be selected by ban veto basis
      4. In case of a draw , Second Overtime will go on for a total of 6 rounds
      5. In case of another draw, overtime games (6 rounds) will go on until a winner is decided
      6. The participants will be switching their team after 5 rounds
    12. The team losing the toss for veto will choose the side they start on (CT or T) Rules Post Prelims :
      1. The number of rounds will be decided depending upon the number of teams remaining after the prelims
      2. The Semi-Final will be a game of 30 rounds (Race to 16)
      3. The Finals will be held for a best of 3 maps and each match will be for 30 rounds
      4. Draw leads to Overtime (6 rounds)

  9. Mock IPL
    1. There will be a preliminary round consisting of an cricket quiz.
    2. 8 teams to be selected for the main round.
    3. Maximum of 2 participants in a team.
    4. Each team will be provided with a stipulated amount of virtual money. This money will be considered as the total budget of the team.
    5. The teams exceeding the budget will be immediately disqualified.
    6. The teams should contain a minimum of 4 batsmen, 4 bowlers, 1 wicketkeeper and 1 allrounder.
    7. The teams which do not satisfy this structure will be disqualified.
    8. The list of players will be provided to each team.
    9. Unsold players can be re-auctioned again at the request of the teams.
    10. The team with the players having the highest aggregate of the rating points will be declared as the winners.
    11. The rating points of the players have been awarded based on the statistics of all the editions of the IPL and also depending on current form, The rating points will be disclosed only at the end of the auction.
