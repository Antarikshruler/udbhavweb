LITERARY EVENT RULES

1. Debate (English) (Inter-College Event)

     Amol : 9945520685

  1. Contestants are to participate alone.
  2. Medium of the debate will be in English.
  3. There will be two rounds, Preliminary and Finals.
  4. Preliminary:
    • Five topics will be given from which the debater will have to choose one.
    • The topics of the debate will be given 24 hours in advance.
    • The debater will be allowed to speak on the topic for a maximum of 3 minutes.
  5. Finals:
    • The topic will be given on the spot.
    • Each debater will be given a preparation time of 10 minutes.
    • Usage of internet and mobile phones are not allowed.
    • The debater will be asked to speak either For or Against the topic.
    • Initially each debater will be allowed to speak on their topic for a maximum of 4 minutes.
    • Debaters will then be allowed to speak on a rebuttal round starting from the speaker who spoke last and ending with the speaker who spoke first.
    • The maximum time of speech allotted for rebuttal round is 2 minutes for each speaker.
    • This will then be followed by a Q/A round which is open to all.
  6. Paper reading is not allowed.

2. Dumb Charades (Inter-College Event)

  1. There must be three contestants in a team.
  2. Contestants may not change/shuffle teams after the prelims.
  3. Number/nature of rounds is up to the DC master.
  4. Contestants may not speak; they may use only established codes. More rules can be specified by the DC master.
  5. Contestants may not use vulgar forms of expressions in the event.
  6. Event will be divided into 2 : Prelims and Finals
  7. Prelims :
    • Team of 3.
    • Up to 2 drops allowed and then you need switch.
    • Will contain movies and TV shows
  8. Finals: Rounds will be decided by the DC master.
  9. Prelims for MSRIT contestants will be held on 21th of March.

3. General Quiz (Inter-College Event)

  1. There will be a written preliminary round and teams will be elected for the final.
  2. A team should consist of 3 people
  3. Finals will be conducted as per quizmaster’s choice.
  4. The specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to reply a particular answer and the type of round will be given before the actual start of the competition

4. Spent Quiz

  1. There will be written preliminary round and the teams will be selected for finals.
  2. A team should consist of 3 people
  3. Finals will be conducted as per quizmaster’s choice.
  4. The specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to reply for a particular answer and the type of round will be given before the actual start of the competition.
  5. Questions will be pertaining to sports and entertainment only.

8. J.A.M (English)

  1. Contestants are allowed to participate alone only.
  2. Medium of expression will be English only.
  3. Number of rounds and number of contestants in each round is up to the jam master.
  4. Contestants may not use vulgar form of expression in the event.

6.India Quiz

  1. There will be a written preliminary round and teams will be elected for the final.
  2. A team should consist a max of 3 people
  3. Finals will be conducted as per quizmaster’s choice.
  4. The specific rules regarding evaluation procedure, time to reply for a particular answer and the type of round will be given before the actual start of the competition.
